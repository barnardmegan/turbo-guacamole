About this page

What is vaginismus?

This is a concept website to raise awareness of vaginismus. Vaginismus is a condition involving a muscle spasm in the pelvic floor muscles. It can make it painful, difficult, or impossible to have sexual intercourse, to undergo a gynecological exam, and to insert a tampon. Despite being a reasonably common condition many people haven not heard of it. If expanded further, this section of the website would detail what vaginismus is and how it is caused.
